Buldak Ramen

Description:

This ramen recipe uses the ever-popular brand of Buldak ramen that has been praised by many for its flavor and spice and ppopularized by k-pop idols on tour. We spruce it up with a bunch of cheese and some hot dogs. And last but not least to any ramen recipe: the soft-boiled eggs! This is perfect for Korean muckbang watching or a rainy day.

Ingredients:

  • 1 packet of Buldak ramen
  • 2-4 Hotdogs
  • a pinch of Minced garlic
  • Quesadilla cheese (or any cheese that melts well)
  • 1/2 a can of corn
  • 1/2 a can of mushrooms
  • 1-4 eggs

Steps:

  1. Boil your eggs for 7 minutes to achieve a geat soft-boiled egg. Then, put them in an ice bath to make peeling easier later on.
  2. Cook the Buldak ramen according to the package instructions.
  3. While the ramen is cooking, heat up your hot dogs.
  4. Chop up your hotdogs and put them into a bowl with the corn, minced garlic, and mushrooms.
  5. When you drain the water from the ramen, turn the heat off and add in your bowl of ingredients and mix it around for 30 seconds to 1 minute.
  6. Add a handful of quesdailla cheese and continue to stir for another 30 seconds to 1 minute.
  7. Take the pot off the heat and add more quesadilla cheese on top (optional) to give it that extra cheesiness!
  8. Pour your ramen into a bowl.
  9. Peel and slice your soft-boiled eggs and place them around the edge of the bowl.
  10. Serve and enjoy!

Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Description:

A childhood classic made just how you like it! For this recipe I'll be using my favorites, but feel free to substitute to your preferences.

Ingredients

  • Creamy Peanut Butter
  • Strawberry Jelly
  • H-E-B Split Top Butter Sliced Bread

Steps:

  1. Spread your peanut butter on one slice of bread
  2. Spread your jelly on the other slice of bread
  3. Close your sandwich and enjoy!

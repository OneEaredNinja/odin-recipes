Turkey Sandwich

Description:

This Turkey Sandwich is the perfect every time of day meal! Whether through necessity of poverty or pickiness, this sandwich never gets old. I will be using my preferences in this recipe, but pick your favorite type of bread, turkey, and cheese to customize this to your taste buds.

Ingredients:

  • H-E-B Split Top Butter Sliced Bread
  • H-E-B brand Honey Turkey
  • H-E-B brand Monterrey Jack cheese with Jalepeno
  • Leafy Lettuce
  • Mustard

Steps:

  1. Place two slices of bread on your plate
  2. Add your lettuce
  3. Add 3-5 slices of Turkey
  4. Add 1 slice of cheese
  5. Spread mustard on the other slice of bread
  6. Assemble sandwich and chow down!
